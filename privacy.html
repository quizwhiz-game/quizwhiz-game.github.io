Privacy Policy

Effective date: February 9, 2026

QuizWhiz ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you use the QuizWhiz mobile application ("the App").

1. Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Display Name: If you choose to set a display name for multiplayer games or leaderboards.
  • Feedback: Any information you voluntarily submit through our feedback portal.

Information Collected Automatically

  • Game Data: Scores, answers, game progress, streaks, and statistics to provide gameplay features like leaderboards and progress tracking.
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, and app version for compatibility and troubleshooting purposes.
  • Multiplayer Session Data: Room codes and match results to facilitate real-time multiplayer games.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain the App's features, including solo play, multiplayer, and leaderboards.
  • Track your game progress and statistics.
  • Improve the App's performance and fix bugs.
  • Respond to your feedback and support requests.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share limited data in the following cases:

  • Multiplayer Games: Your display name and game scores are visible to other players in the same game room.
  • Leaderboards: Your display name and scores may appear on public leaderboards within the App.
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or to protect our rights.

4. Data Retention

Game progress and statistics are stored locally on your device. If you uninstall the App, this data is deleted. Multiplayer session data is temporary and is not retained after the game ends.

5. Children's Privacy

QuizWhiz is a trivia game suitable for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us so we can promptly delete it.

6. Third-Party Services

The App is distributed through the Google Play Store, which is governed by Google's Privacy Policy. We may use third-party services for analytics or crash reporting, each governed by their own privacy policies.

7. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the information we collect. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Your Rights

You can:

  • Delete your local game data by uninstalling the App.
  • Request information about data we hold about you.
  • Request deletion of any data associated with you.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, you can reach us through our feedback portal or by email at akpulatugurcan@gmail.com.
